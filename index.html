TweetPortfolio: Index Help

Welcome to TweetPortfolio! Thank you for using our service!

New Users: Just click the search button, enter your query, and press enter! The results will look very similar to your Twitter Feed, and you can add Tweets to your Folders. If you have additional questions, there is more help on the main page.

Returning Users: You know the drill! If you want to resume your previous session, press the resume session button. If you are interested in an existing trending topic, click on it to search it automatically. Thanks for being a loyal user!
